Before You Leave the House

Morning Send-Off Checklist

  • Apply sunscreen (face, arms, neck)
  • Pack a full water bottle
  • Pack a sack lunch — no lunch provided at Good Shepherd or golf camp
  • Sunscreen re-apply spray in backpack for outdoor days
  • Weather-appropriate layers (MN mornings can be cool)
  • Any medication if needed
  • Check backpack for permission slips / field trip notes

Good to Know

Lunches

Pack a Lunch Daily

Neither Good Shepherd Summer Care nor the golf camp provide lunch. Pack a sack lunch for both Rae and Ivy every day.

Sunscreen

Apply Before Drop-Off

Good Shepherd takes kids on outdoor field trips regularly (Minnesota Zoo, outdoor activities). Apply sunscreen at home before the car ride. Pack a spray bottle in Rae's bag for reapplication.

Field Trips

Good Shepherd Summer Care

Includes 3–4 field trips per week. Trips include the Minnesota Zoo, Pump It Up, a Lynx game, and a Twins game. Cost is included — no extra money needed.
