Good to Know — Every Day

Pack a lunch — nut-free

No lunch is provided. Lunches must be peanut & tree-nut free. No microwave, so nothing that needs heating. Pack silverware if needed.

Sunscreen before drop-off

Apply every day before you bring Rae. School uses Up & Up SPF 50 to reapply — send your own if she's sensitive to it.

Tennis shoes — no flip-flops

Lots of gym & outdoor time. Flip-flops aren't allowed. Send closed-toe shoes; a labeled water bottle & a book are good every day.

Pickup by 5:30 — late fee

After 5:30 it's $30 per 15 minutes. Drop off no later than 9:30am (earlier on field-trip days so Rae doesn't miss the bus).
